About Virtues Land College

Welcome to Virtues Land College, where we are committed to raising superstars! Located in the heart of Egan-Igando, Lagos, we provide world-class education from primary to secondary levels.

Our holistic approach combines academic excellence with character development, technology training, and practical skills. We prepare students not just for exams, but for life in the 21st century.

With our modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories and comprehensive coding training programs, we ensure every student has access to cutting-edge education that prepares them for future success.

Our Programs

Comprehensive educational programs designed to nurture every aspect of student development

Primary Education

Strong foundation in core subjects with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and character building. Our primary section nurtures young minds in a safe and stimulating environment.

Secondary Education

Comprehensive secondary curriculum preparing students for WAEC, NECO, and university entrance. Focus on academic excellence and holistic development.

Coding & Technology

Learn programming, web development, robotics, and computer science. Preparing students for the digital economy with practical tech skills and projects.

Science Laboratory

Hands-on experiments in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Our modern lab facilities make science come alive for students with practical learning.

Sports & Athletics

Chase, football, athletics, and more. Developing physical fitness, teamwork, discipline and sportsmanship in every student.

Character Development

Building virtues, leadership skills, and moral values. We raise not just scholars, but well-rounded superstars ready to make positive impact.
